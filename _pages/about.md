I am Wenbin Huang, a third-year undergraduate student in Computer Science and Technology at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/), where I am part of the Zhiyuan Honors Program ACM Class. I expect to receive my B.S. degree in June 2027.

I am currently a research intern at [X-LANCE](https://x-lance.github.io/), advised by [Prof. Kai Yu](https://x-lance.github.io/kaiyu/). My research interests include speech intelligence, AudioLLM, audio-text understanding, and automatic speech recognition.

After graduation, I plan to apply to Ph.D. programs in related areas in North America, Singapore, and Hong Kong. Outside research, I enjoy games, music, animation, and good food. Feel free to contact me.
